purpose


Our purpose is to increase the quality of education for all elementary students in Utah. We do this by providing educational material and supplies to underfunded schools. Then, we work with teachers to set meaningful goals for their students. If the classroom reaches their goal by the end of the year, PIZZA'S ON US!

Services


We serve northern and central Utah schools, prioritizing low-income areas. Teachers are given an opportunity to request any supplies they feel their classroom needs. Once all schools have finalized their requests, we buy the supplies in bulk from trusted sellers who give us the best deal. Finally, when we return with the supplies, we meet with each teacher to establish a reasonable classroom goal. Once the class has reached that goal we return with pizza, soda, and music.

Impact



Utah is currently #5 in the U.S. in educational quality. Despite our top-notch educators most school supplies come out of the pockets of teachers. With low wages and tough jobs, this puts unnecessary strain on the educational system. With your help, we can ease this burden and brighten the classroom.

While helping teachers is crucial, students are just as important. To help motivate kids, we offer paid pizza parties. Music and food were the best part of school for a lot of us, and it’s a great way to connect with other students in your grade. We believe that a classroom with all the supplies they need and happy students performs better.
